About Us

We started out in a basement as a project among friends, but quickly grew and worked our way up to the facility that we have today. Our mission is to bring games to those who don't have the money to buy a gaming rig outright, and provide an outlet for pals to get together and have fun... or turn eachother into enemies. (We also sell tacos!)

When were we founded?

Quite a while ago! August 1995 to be specific :-)
